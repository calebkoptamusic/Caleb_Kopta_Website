About

Caleb Kopta is a Pittsburgh native multifaceted pop artist inspired by the greats: David Bowie, Paul McCartney, Bruce Springsteen, The Killers, and newcomers like Harry Styles and Charlie Puth. He isn’t concerned about genres, he just listens to what feels good and in return, makes what feels good. Kopta wants to tell a story that is true to himself and also resonates with his audience.
